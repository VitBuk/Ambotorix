HersonDraft rules:

1.Banned are Tokugawa, Cyrus, Nader Shah, and Cree
2.All players must submit 4 picks to the tournament organiser
3.Please label these picks 1, 2, 3, and 4. With your first choice being pick 1
4.Any picks chosen by 2 or more players will be banned, and 2nd priority picks then chosen for players who selected banned civs
5.This process will repeat until all players have unique civs.
If there are still duplicates after 4 choices the tournament organiser will flip to determine who receives their 4th pick,
and the other player will be asked to pick again from the pool of remaining civs
6.Picks will be hidden until the game has ended, so feel free to pick your comfort picks
